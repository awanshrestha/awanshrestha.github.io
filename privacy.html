Sharing of Your Information We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the App's functionality or as required by law. 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the App or through other means, such as email. 7. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@tastemeapp.com.
